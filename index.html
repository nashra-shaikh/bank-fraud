Understanding Online Fraud

Online fraud includes a variety of deceptive activities aimed at stealing personal or financial information through digital means. It encompasses phishing scams, identity theft, and fraudulent transactions.

Importance of Digital Security

Ensuring digital security is vital for protecting your sensitive information and maintaining your online privacy. Neglecting online security can lead to unauthorized access, data breaches, and financial losses.
